About

I've been a resident of this city for the majority of my life and a homeowner in District 18 for the last decade. I truly enjoy living here and I want my efforts as a Councillor to have a positive impact on the city. Please contact me with any ideas you might have to improve the municipality.

Education

I completed my Honours degree in Public Administration and Political Science (BsocSc) from the University of Ottawa followed by a Master of Public Administration (MPA) from Western University.

Career

I began my career working with the Commissioner of Community Services and, after many excellent years, I resigned to pursue municipal politics. I've since had the pleasure of serving as the councillor for District 18 for over 8 years. I serve on both the Administrative Policies and Planning committees.
